Why Law Made Simple?

Thousands of men in India face false accusations every year. Dense legal language keeps them from understanding their own rights — the very rights that exist to protect them.

This tool breaks down every Indian law into plain language. So any man can walk into a courtroom — or a police station — knowing exactly what the law says and what it means for him.
